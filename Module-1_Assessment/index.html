About Me

My name is Kelton Holsen and I am a recent graduate majoring in computer science and creative writing. Yes, it's an interesting combination. I pride myself on my ability to adapt to new circumstances using my analytical skills. In my free time, I run tabletop roleplaying games like Dungeons and Dragons, sing, and occasionally make mead.

A fun fact about me is that, in the fourth grade, I was given an award by my school for having the most time logged on StudyIsland.com. For this accomplishment, I was given a men's XL StudyIsland t-shirt which I still own to this day (it still doesn't fit). I'm about 90% certain that it was sent to the principal by the StudyIsland people and he decided to give it as a prize instead. I also got to play in a live-action version of Deal or No Deal in which I inadvertantly turned down a $100 bill, but won 250 pieces of gum.
